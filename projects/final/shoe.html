Origami

Shoe

All you need to make this shoe is a single piece of paper; it does not matter if it is square or rectangle, you'll just end up with a boot if you use a rectangular piece of paper. If you want an interesting and easy party favor, you can use well-starched cloth napkins and fold boots for your guests! If you want to make a pair of shoes, simply switch which side you fold up when you're creating the back of the other shoe.
