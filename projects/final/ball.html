Spiky Ball

The fold for this origami is not difficult at all; what makes this a more challenging piece is when it comes time to put it all together. It can be easy for the paper to slip and come loose while you're trying to connect them, or to become confused as to what piece goes where. A suggestion, which is also stated in the video, is to focus on creating triangles. As long as you're creating those three-point tips, this origami will begin to take shape!
