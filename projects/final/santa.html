Origami

Santa Claus

Technically, this piece is considered kirigami rather than origami. This is due to the fact it's not a three-dimensional fold, and you also have to glue paper together to keep them in place--otherwise santa's head would be falling off all the time! However, it adds some variety to this small collection of origami lessons; and who doesn't like Santa!!! Make a lot of these to remind people in your life that Santa is always watching.
