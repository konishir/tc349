Origami

Box

This origami pretty simple to put together, but keep in mind it takes 6 pieces of paper. Once you've completed your cube, though, options are limitless! Create replacement die for ones you've lost; secretly store things inside, all you need to do is un-tuck a flap or a two. Personalize them and give them away as gifts! Never thought it'd be so useful to know how to make paper boxes, right?
