Origami

Frog

This origami frog also functions as a toy; when you're done creating it, you'll be able to make it leap into the air! It is not too complicated to make, so you can go ahead and create an entire army of leaping frogs. Try experimenting with different thicknesses of paper to see how that affects the power of the jump. Smaller pieces of paper could also have the potential of creating a springier leap!
